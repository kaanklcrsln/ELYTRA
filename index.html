ELYTRA — Satellite Tracker

A real-time dashboard for every country with objects in orbit. Positions and orbital elements are propagated client-side from live TLE data (CelesTrak), cross-referenced with the SATCAT registry for ownership.

Pick a country tab, tick satellites, and watch them on the 2D map, the 3D orbit view and the live comparison charts simultaneously. Click any row to inspect its full TLE-derived element set on the left.
